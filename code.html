Sampling from the gamcon distribution of Type 1

Samples from the GamCon distribution of Type 1 of Damsleth (1975) employing the algorithm of Devroye (2012). This distribution is conjugate for the shape parameter of the Gamma distribution with known scale. Programming language is Matlab/Octave. Download.

Sampling a from non-absorving Squared Bessel Bridge

Simulates an exact path of a non-absorving Square Bessel Bridge at a predefined time grid. Includes an implementation of the first algorithm of Devroye (2002) for Bessel random variables. Can also be employed to sample from other bridges for processes with known transformations to the Squared Bessel process. Programming language is Matlab/Octave. Download
