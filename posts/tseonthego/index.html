Smooth as consumption

Time series Econometrics on the go

January 22, 2018

A Shiny app for easy and on the go univariate time series econometrics. No installation required, just upload your data and start forecasting after a few clicks!

The models available are:

  • Exponential Smoothing (ETS) model
  • Autoregressive iterated moving average (ARIMA) model

Check it out at: https://llucpuigcodina.shinyapps.io/TSEonthego/ and https://github.com/LlucPuigCodina/TSEonthego
