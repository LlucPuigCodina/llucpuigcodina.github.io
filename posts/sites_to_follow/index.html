Sites to follow

  • RBlogs: R news and tutorials contributed by R bloggers.

  • StatsBlog: StatsBlogs syndicates posts from statistics related blogs and brings traffic and user interaction to contributing blogs.

  • VoxEU: CEPR’s policy portal to promote research-based policy analysis and commentary by leading economists.

  • EconLog: Blog of the Library of Economics and Liberty.

  • Nada es Gratis: El mejor blog de economía en español.

  • Els Opuscles del CREI The instrument for the diffusion of CREI’s research beyond the academic world.

  • MarginalRevolution: Blog focused on economics run by economists Tyler Cowen and Alex Tabarrok.

  • Pseudoerasmus: blog (mostly) about economic history, growth, and development.

  • Nintil: The Internet’s finest blog.

  • The Leisure of the Theory Class: Blog devoted to speculation and ruminations on all aspects of economic and game theory.

  • Gwern: Website of Gwern Branwen, writer & independent researcher.

  • Slate Star Codex: a blog about science, medicine, philosophy, politics, and futurism.

  • MIT Technology Review

  • Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy

  • XKCD: A webcomic of romance, sarcasm, math, and language

  • SMBC: Saturday Morning Breakfast Cereal (webcomic).
