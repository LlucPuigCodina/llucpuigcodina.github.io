Smooth as consumption

Determinacy in a New Keynesian Model under a Money Growth Rule

July 18, 2017

This note derives the analytical conditions for determinacy in a canonical New Keynesian model under an exogenous path for the growth rate of the money supply. Under cash-when-I’m-done timing the model is always determinate while indeterminacy can arise with cash-in-advance timing.
